Every organisation runs on five questions: what's happening, what's about to go
wrong, why, what to do, and what it just learned. Most tools only answer the
first — a record of what already happened.

## The shift

Dashboards are rear-view mirrors. The useful thing is a clear, honest read of
what's coming, early enough to act on it.
